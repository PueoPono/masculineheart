Archetypal Masculine Heart Quest is a 21-day guided journey into the heart, story, symbol, masculinity, intention, and lived vitality.

This is not just a video series. It is a descent into deeper terrain — one day at a time — to help you reconnect with the living intelligence of the heart and the hidden roots beneath habit, identity, and emotional patterning.

The Masculine Heart Quest brings together three streams of work into one guided progression: opening the heart, learning the language of story and symbol, and planting deeper intentions into the heart and subconscious.

  • short video lessons with grounded pacing
  • reflection prompts to invite the heart to wake up and feel
  • completion-based 24-hour unlock rhythm
  • at your pace
  • optional reminder support by email or text

The quest unfolds in three arcs.

Each arc invites a different descent: into emotional truth, into story and symbol, and into the intentional planting of what you want to become.

Days 1–7

Unlock the Heart

A return to feeling, honesty, and the emotional truths that shape life beneath the surface.

Days 8–14

Language of the Heart

A journey through Iron John, symbol, story, identity, shadow, and the deeper architecture of masculinity.

Days 15–21

Intentions Worth Planting

Planting qualities into the heart and subconscious so the path continues in lived form.
